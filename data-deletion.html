Data Deletion Request

This page is for users of Evil Doll by Bonobo Games who wish to request the deletion of their personal data, in accordance with data protection laws such as GDPR or CCPA.

What Data Is Collected?

Our game uses third-party ad networks that may collect the following types of data to serve ads:

  • Advertising ID (Google Ad ID or Apple IDFA)
  • Device details (model, OS version, language)
  • IP address (used to estimate general location)
  • App usage data (e.g., time spent, interactions)

The following ad networks may collect and process this data:

  • ironSource (via LevelPlay)
  • Unity Ads
  • Google AdMob
  • Mintegral
  • Meta Audience Network

How to Request Data Deletion

If you would like us to delete your data, please email us at:

Email: roydevbusiness@gmail.com

In your email, please include:

  • Your device's Advertising ID (Google Ad ID or Apple IDFA)
  • Your device model (e.g., iPhone 13, Samsung Galaxy A51)
  • The name of the app: Evil Doll

We will respond within 30 days and confirm once your data has been removed or anonymized.
